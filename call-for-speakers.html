Speaker Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in speaking at Iterate.PHX!

One of the main goals behind Iterate.PHX is to foster a more-vibrant and connected community, so please let us know about improvements we can make.

Presentation Content

Presentations should try to increase the motiviation and ability of attendees to apply what you are talking-about in their own organizations.

Please try to explain:

  • why the problem being solved is important
  • how the problem was solved using the demonstrated tech with focus-on the general approach to the solution and why it works
    • giving folks enough context to apply the solution to their own environment
    • we are trying to avoid solutions that simplify to "buy commercial service X" or amount to product pitches; however, sessions that explain the journey to commercial service X and the resulting transformation of the organization will be considered
  • unsolved problems and/or opportunities in the space
Time Management

Sessions are scheduled 45 minutes apart, so speakers should plan to keep topic presentations to 40 minutes or less.

Recommended time buckets

  • 30-35 minutes of presentation and demonstration
  • 10-5 minutes of questions & answers
  • 5 minutes of teardown & setup for next session

Please encourage interaction with the attendees throughout the session by, e.g. encouraging questions throughout the presentation and demo.
